ARTIFACT 2

Journal Article

File Name: Journal Article.pdf

Open Journal Article PDF

Title: AI-Based Road Surface and Damage Detection with Condition Scoring Using YOLO

This artifact contains our journal article about an AI-based road condition assessment system. The project uses YOLO models to classify road surface types, detect road damages such as cracks, potholes, and manholes, and generate a road condition score. The system was also deployed as a Streamlit web application for easier image testing and demonstration.

Contribution to CpE Student Outcomes

This artifact contributes to my Computer Engineering student outcomes by showing my ability to conduct research, analyze a real-world problem, and document a technology-based solution. It also helped me apply concepts in artificial intelligence, computer vision, model evaluation, and software deployment.

Contribution to Personal Goals

This artifact helped me improve my research writing, technical explanation, and project documentation skills. It also gave me more experience in presenting an AI project in a professional way.
